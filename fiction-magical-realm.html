by J.R.R. Tolkien

Plot-Synopsis

Bilbo Baggins, a hobbit with no interest in adventures, is
unexpectedly swept into an epic quest. Together with dwarves
and a wizard, he seeks to reclaim a lost treasure guarded by
the fearsome dragon Smaug.

Stardust
by Neil Gaiman

Plot-Synopsis

Tristran Thorn ventures into the magical land of Faerie
to retrieve a fallen star for his beloved. Along the way, he

A tale blending adventure and enchantment.

Plot-Synopsis

An adventurous geology professor chances upon a manuscript in which a 16th-century explorer claims to have found a route to the

Iceland in the company of Hans Bjelke, a native guide.
