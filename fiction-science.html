The Time Machine
by H. G. Wells

Plot-Synopsis

A Victorian scientist invents a machine to travel through time.
He explores distant future eras and witnesses the rise and fall
of civilizations, encountering both wonder and danger in the
evolution of humanity.

Twenty Thousand Leagues Under the Seas
by Jules Verne

Plot-Synopsis

Captain Nemo commands the Nautilus, a submarine capable of
extraordinary feats. Together with a captive crew, he explores
the ocean's depths, uncovering its beauty, secrets, and perils.
From coral forests to shipwrecks, the journey is both
breathtaking and mysterious.

From the Earth to the Moon
by Jules Verne

Plot-Synopsis

A group of American scientists and engineers plan a daring
mission to launch a projectile to the moon. With ingenuity
and determination, they construct an enormous cannon.
The story captures their adventures, challenges, and
boundless curiosity about space exploration.
